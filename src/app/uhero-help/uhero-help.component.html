About

The UHERO Data Portal provides the Hawaii community with information on economic, demographic, and business trends in the State and the Asia-Pacific region. It includes a wide array of economic and social indicators for the State of Hawaii, the four counties, and key external economies.

About the Data

This unique database, which is continually expanding, currently houses historical data on more than 3,000 economic indicators. Much of the data reported in the system comes from government data sources; some originates at UHERO. Source notes are provided for each data series.

UHERO makes no warranty, either express or implied, as to the accuracy or usability of the information in the Data Portal. We encourage you to report errors and give us your suggestions for improvement.

Using the UHERO Data Portal

Click on the categories on the left to change which group of time series are displayed.

Chart View

Drop-down selectors indicate the currently displayed region and frequency. Use these drop-down selectors to change the display from “State of Hawaii”, “Annual” to “Honolulu County”, “Quarterly”. By moving the sliders at the top of the Chart View, you can adjust the range of displayed periods. You can also switch to Table View.

Each chart in the main Chart View includes the name of the series, the date of the currently highlighted observation, the value (with units in parenthesis), and the year-over-year change (or percent change). You can hover your mouse cursor over each series to see the values at different points in time. Clicking on a series chart will open up the single-series view.

Table View

In Table View, you can toggle the display of the year-over-year change (or percent change). If more periods are selected than can be displayed at once, you can scroll the table horizontally to display the remaining observations. Click the “Download CSV” link to download the data in a format that loads in Excel.

Series View

When viewing a single series, the line indicates the observations and the bars indicate the year-over-year change (or percent change). Beside the series title you can use the drop-down selectors to change the displayed region and frequency. You can also toggle whether the seasonally adjusted series or the raw series is displayed when both are available.

On the top-left of the chart there are links to change the displayed periods to include the last year, the last five years, the last ten years, or all available data. You can also use the controls on the smaller chart below the main chart to manually adjust the displayed range. The smaller chart will always display the entire range.

On the top-right of the chart area, click “Print” to print the current chart, or click “Download” to choose from PNG, JPEG, SVG, PDF or CSV exports of the selected data.

Below the chart is listed the source of the data, a link to the source agency, and any relevant notes. Below the series metadata we display summary statistics of the currently selected range. You can toggle the date order of the observations in the table below.

Search

Type a keyword into the search box at the top to see a list of all series with that keyword in the series title or in the name of the series category.
